What are Blockchain Tokens?

Tokens in a blockchain represent digital assets or utilities that can be transferred between addresses. They can represent:

  • Cryptocurrencies: Digital money like Btc or Ether
  • Assets: Representation of physical assets like real estate or gold
  • Utility: Access rights to a service or platform
  • Security: Digital representation of traditional securities

Tokens are tracked on the blockchain through transactions that record transfers from one address to another.

This is a simplified demonstration of token transactions on a blockchain. In a real blockchain:

  • Transactions would be signed with private keys
  • Transactions would be grouped into blocks
  • Blocks would be mined and added to the blockchain
  • The entire network would validate and agree on transactions
